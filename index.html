Our Vision

A leading light in Islamic education

To be a leading Islamic educational institution dedicated to nurturing knowledgeable, morally upright, and responsible individuals who excel academically while upholding the teachings of Islam for the benefit of humanity.

Our Mission

Education rooted in Qur'an & Sunnah

To provide quality education rooted in the Qur'an, Sunnah, and sound academic standards — instilling taqwa, good character, and balanced intellectual, spiritual, social, and physical development in every student.

What We Offer

Programs & Pathways

A balanced curriculum that honours Islamic tradition while equipping students for the world — from Qur'anic memorisation to contemporary academic excellence.

Qur'an Memorisation (Hifdh)

Structured hifdhul Qur'an programme with qualified huffadh, tajweed instruction, and regular revision sessions.

Islamic Studies

Comprehensive study of Fiqh, Aqeedah, Seerah, Hadith sciences, and Arabic language from qualified scholars.

Academic Education

Core academic subjects aligned to national standards — mathematics, sciences, English, and social studies.

Character Development

Dedicated pastoral care, Islamic ethics workshops, community service, and mentorship for holistic growth.
